# Platform Commercialization and Service Packaging Guide

## Purpose

This document defines how to package the synthetic Microsoft 365 E5 / Microsoft Purview banking and financial-services simulation platform into reusable commercial offerings, workshops, advisory services, demos, training programs, and managed service motions.

It is designed to help convert a technical cyber-range and governance simulation framework into business conversations, Microsoft-aligned engagements, customer workshops, and service revenue opportunities.

All demos, telemetry, personas, customer records, financial data, HR records, legal matters, and incidents referenced by the platform remain fictional and synthetic.

---

## Commercial Positioning

The platform should be positioned as a reusable Microsoft 365 governance, data security, and AI readiness accelerator.

Core commercial message:

> We help organizations understand how sensitive data moves through Microsoft 365, how AI changes the exposure model, and how Microsoft Purview, Defender, Entra, and Copilot governance can reduce risk while enabling business productivity.

Supporting message:

> This is not just a demo. It is a repeatable way to move from awareness to assessment, roadmap, implementation, and managed improvement.

## Demo-to-Engagement Conversion Questions

Use these after a demo:

1. Which scenario felt closest to your environment?
2. Which data domain would cause the highest impact if overshared?
3. Do you know where that data lives today?
4. Are sensitivity labels already deployed?
5. Are DLP overrides reviewed today?
6. Are users allowed to use external AI tools?
7. Is Copilot already enabled or planned?
8. Are endpoint print/copy activities visible?
9. Who owns external sharing governance?
10. Would leadership value a dashboard like this for monthly review?

## Standard Assumptions

Use assumptions such as:

- customer provides required administrative access
- customer validates production policy decisions
- all production controls are tested before enforcement
- DLP policies may start in audit or test mode
- customer identifies business data owners
- HR, Legal, and privacy stakeholders validate user-risk workflows
- Microsoft licensing and feature availability must be confirmed
- Microsoft-funded program eligibility is not guaranteed until validated

---

## Standard Exclusions

Potential exclusions:

- production incident response
- forensic investigation
- legal advice
- HR disciplinary process design
- guaranteed compliance certification
- full data remediation without separate scope
- migration of all legacy repositories
- custom application development unless separately scoped
- managed service unless explicitly included
