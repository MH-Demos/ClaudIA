# Managed Services Operating Model and Recurring Governance Review Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the recurring-services and long-term customer-operating-model layer for the synthetic Microsoft 365 E5 / Microsoft Purview banking and financial-services simulation platform.

It explains how the demo, assessment, and roadmap assets can evolve into managed governance services covering Microsoft Purview, DLP, Endpoint DLP, Copilot and AI governance, external sharing, SOC review, executive reporting, and continuous improvement.

All examples, metrics, incidents, users, files, prompts, identifiers, customers, HR records, legal matters, financial records, and telemetry used by the platform remain fictional and synthetic unless a separate governed customer process is explicitly established.

---

## Core Managed Services Thesis

> The value of a managed data-security service is not only reviewing alerts. The value is creating a recurring governance rhythm that turns telemetry into decisions, decisions into remediation, and remediation into measurable maturity improvement.

A managed governance service should combine:

```text
visibility
control tuning
business context
executive reporting
user coaching
risk reduction
continuous improvement
```

---

## Managed Service Objectives

Primary objectives:

1. Maintain visibility over sensitive data movement.
2. Review and tune DLP outcomes.
3. Monitor AI and Copilot governance risk.
4. Review external sharing and guest access exposure.
5. Monitor endpoint movement of sensitive content.
6. Support SOC/data-security triage workflows.
7. Produce executive-ready governance reporting.
8. Maintain a prioritized remediation backlog.
9. Reassess maturity quarterly.
10. Improve safe collaboration and AI adoption over time.

## Managed Detection and Governance Integration

The service should integrate detection operations with governance operations.

Detection operations answer:

```text
What happened?
Which signals appeared?
Which users, files, apps, devices, and domains were involved?
```

Governance operations answer:

```text
Why did it happen?
Was the workflow unclear?
Was the safe path available?
Should the policy change?
Should the user be coached?
Should ownership or permissions change?
```

## Safety Reminder

Managed service operations using this framework must follow customer-approved legal, privacy, HR, compliance, and security governance processes.

Do not use synthetic demo assets as production evidence. Do not store real customer data, real employee data, real HR records, real legal matters, real financial data, real credentials, real secrets, real production telemetry, or real incident evidence in the synthetic demo repository.
